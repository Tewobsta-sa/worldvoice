Digital Scout Program

Empowering students and teachers to become leaders in the digital age.

Our Digital Scout program empowers students and teachers to become leaders in the digital age. This comprehensive program fosters digital citizenship by teaching responsible online behavior, ethical decision-making, and safe internet practices. Recognizing the importance of bridging the digital divide, we provide access to technology and digital literacy training, ensuring equitable opportunities for all participants.

The program ignites a passion for innovation through hands-on projects, encouraging creative problem-solving and entrepreneurial thinking. Our dedicated STEM Lab provides a space for exploration and experimentation, allowing scouts to delve into science, technology, engineering, and mathematics through engaging activities and real-world applications, preparing them for future success in STEM fields.

Digital Citizenship

Digital Literacy

We equip participants with the skills to critically evaluate online information, identify misinformation, and effectively use digital tools for learning and communication.

Digital Safety

Our program teaches practical strategies for staying safe online, including strong passwords, protecting personal information, and recognizing online scams.

Digital Participation

We encourage active and responsible engagement in online communities, teaching participants how to use digital tools for civic engagement and constructive participation.
